Jojo's Bizarre Adventure

This anime has a very "bizarre" story. It doesn't follow the conventional anime plot, and it mainly focuses on having a good/weird/adventurous moment instead of following a logical story. The main characters of the anime are the Joestar family. Each season focuses on the the adventure of one individual in this family. Also, all the seasons have a very diffrent style, tone, and vibe from each other. This because, each adventure of a certain individual is a greatly different experience.
